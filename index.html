Welcome to Gnoun's official website!

Nice to see you here! On this website, everything is made from scratch by Gnoun. My most notable projects are my music, originally composed by me, and various math creations, notably the graphs. I've also created a set of math tools which, unlike many other, are easy and comfortable to use. Other projects include stories, piano improvisations, and more. If you want to be up to date, make sure to visit the What's up section. If you want to read the website in another language, select one from the Language Selection on the left. You can also check the links on the right; apart from my YouTube channel and Discord server, they include my game development team page and some of my cooperators' pages. Enjoy your stay!

About Gnoun

I'm a young "mathemusician" and I do some math every day. My favorite math topics include calculus, number theory, complex numbers and differential equations. The way Desmos works made me interested in yet another part of math, which is graphing. I've also started creating music in 2019 and I've made a few dozen pieces which are mostly of moderate quality. I make music in FL Studio. I'm also really interested in the English language, which is not my native language, and in languages in general. Sometimes I also do some programming, but I don't consider myself a good programmer.
